Vegan Brownies

Delicious and Gooey Vegan Brownies

These irresistable treats are made with entirely vegan ingredients, flavored with gems and minerals from the doomed Mines of Moria after the fall of the Dwarven Race

Ingredients

  • Artichoke Hearts
  • Eye of Newt vegan substitute
  • The Arkenstone

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F
  2. Cross the perilous mountain path into the heart of Erebor, where you must vanquish the evil Smaug and steal the treasured Arkenstone from the mighty Thorin Oakenshield
  3. Combine all ingredients together and bake for 3 hours
