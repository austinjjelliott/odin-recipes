Plain and Simple White Bread

This easy and versatile recipe will have delicious, fresh bread ready to eat in mere hours. With a few easy changes, this recipe can be tweaked into a simple focaccia, buns, or even a beautiful plaited loaf

Ingredients

  • 1 lb flour
  • 1 TBSP Oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp yeast
  • 1/2 TBSP salt
  • 10 oz warm water

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients in mixing bowl for several mintues, until dough forms
  2. Shape into ball and proof for 2 hours at 80 degrees F
  3. Knead by hand for just a few minutes and form into desired shape
  4. Proof again for 1 hour at 80 degrees F
  5. Bake at 400 degrees F for 25 minutes
