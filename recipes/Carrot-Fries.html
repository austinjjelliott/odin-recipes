Carrot Fries

Eh...What's up, Doc

Carrot Fries are chopped up carrots (in fry shape) spritzed with oil and spices make them resemble French Fries"

Ingredients

  • Carrots
  • Oil
  • Any Seasoning You Want

Steps

  1. Chop up the Carrots into french fry shape
  2. Spritz with oil and toss with spices
  3. Air Fry at 400 degrees for 17 minutes
